Tips and Tricks!

Being a good baker, takes precision and technique. To help out beginning bakers I have made a list of the best baking tips in which I have learned from trial and error.

Follow these tips to become a star baker!

Heart shaped baking molds for Valentine's Day!

  • Invest in a silpat, this silicone baking sheet can be used in replace of parchment paper. Not only is it good for the enviornment, as it is reusable, it has great non-stick coating that ensures your baked goods will come out intact!
  • There is a difference between regular rubber spatulas and heat proof rubber spautlas. Protip: always go for heat proof...trust me on this one.
  • Always coat mix ins like nuts, chocolate chips, berries in flour to prevent them from sinking to the bottom of you dish. Who wants a banana bread with chocolate only on the bottom when you can have it throoughout?
  • This may seem like an obvious suggestion but, ALWAYS read the recipe in full before you start or you may miss some steps and ruin your treat.
  • Measuring matters! Even if you think you are pretty good at eyeballing measurements...do NOT! Like I mentioned before, baking is a science and you must follow the recipe to a T.

  • Adding instant coffee or instant espresso powder brings out the chocolate flavor in any dish.
